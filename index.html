how much does house cleaning actually cost?

The national average cost of house cleaning services is $40-$65 per hour per cleaner.

House cleaning cost per square foot:

House cleaning companies can charge differently, but most charge by the hour. It’s also common to see house cleaners charge by number of bedrooms and bathrooms. Others charge by the square foot. Depending on the service they are providing, a company may charge different rates per square foot. The average price per square foot is $0.07 to $0.12 per square foot. scope of work

deep clean

The Deep Clean involves wet wiping of all the doors, cabinet fronts, baseboards, blinds, all light fixtures, fronts and sides of dressers and nightstands, all reachable ceiling fans, top of refrigerator, and inside oven.

It also includes cleaning of all bathrooms, which include all sinks, showers, tubs, toilets, vanitys and mirrors. Kitchen, all countertops, inside and out microwave, outside of toaster, and outside of oven. Bedrooms, dusting tops of nightstands, top of dressers, making of all the beds, and changing linens on only one bed of clients choosing. Living room, dust all end tables, and dust top of mantel. This includes upstairs and downstairs. Also vacuum and mop all rooms.

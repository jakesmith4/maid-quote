how much does house cleaning actually cost?

The national average cost of house cleaning services is $40-$65 per hour per cleaner.

House cleaning cost per square foot:

House cleaning companies can charge differently, but most charge by the hour. It’s also common to see house cleaners charge by number of bedrooms and bathrooms. Others charge by the square foot. Depending on the service they are providing, a company may charge different rates per square foot. The average price per square foot is $0.07 to $0.12 per square foot. scope of work

general/maintenance clean

  • wool dust window sills, ledges and blinds.
  • wool dust woodwork, such as shelves, baseboards, doors, light fixtures and ceiling fans.
  • wool dust or hand-wipe picture frames and knickknacks.
  • Polish furniture with polish - move all items from the furniture to be polished.
  • remove cobwiebs both high and low.
  • clean light switch plates for finger smudges.
  • wastebaskets emptied/relined and clean ash trays.
  • clean all glass doors and sidelights - front and back doors.
  • vacuum all carpets & rugs (or shake). Vacuum stairs. Move lightweight items when vacuuming.
  • vacuum and mop all hard floors accordingly. move area rugs to mop beneath.
  • make all beds, changing the linens on one bed.
  • wipe down exercise equipment.
  • dust or sweep the return air vents and regular air vents in all the rooms.
  • pick up toys and place in a toy-box (not categorizing them, but just pick up and straighten).
  • wipe down laundry machines, including the interior rim of the doors.
  • shake out dog beds & vacuum underneath. move dog crates to clean floors around & under.
  • offices: don't move bills or documents. pick them up and wipe beneath them. straighten area.
  • finishing touches: fold throw-blankets; straighten couch pillows, counter top trinkets, magazines, remote contorls, ect. make everything look clean and straight at first glance
bathrooms
  • move everything from vanity and wipe down. Scrub sinks.
  • move items in shower. scrub shower, making sure to clean soap dishes. wipe shower dry an put everything back.
  • scrub inside of toilet. clean and disinfect tank and top of tank, under the rim, seats (on top and underneath), back of toilet, handle, and bottom of toilet. wipe dry
  • polish mirrors and scrub around any fixtures, polishing them.
  • dust all light fixtures.
  • refold all towels and hand towels. fold toilet paper to a point.
  • shake/vacuum area rugs.
  • straighten any book, magazines, and toys kept in here
kitchens
  • clean and disinfect countertops.
  • clean range top. remove grills of gas ranges (or drip pans) and wipe clean.
  • clean and disinfect microwave inside and out.
  • polish exposed sides of appliances and counter decor. sweep & mop under the front edges.
  • wash all items that are on the counter (small appliances/pictures, ect).
  • empty toaster crumbs and coffee grounds from machines.
  • clean and disinfect basksplashes of counter. scrub sink, and wipe dry.
  • dishes: if the sink is overflowing - skip the sink and maybe leave behind a note. if there are a few then put them in dishwasher. if dishwasher is full or "clean", place rinsed dishes back in clean sink.
  • move all dining chairs and barstools to vacuum and mop underneath.
  • dust top of the refrigerator.

deep clean

  • every deep clean includes the general/maintenance cleaning list, with detailed emphasis on buildup, PLUS the following:
  • hand wash or wipe all ceiling fan blades (reachable).
  • wash inside of reachable window sills and ledges.
  • clean interior glass doors and sidelights inside and out (such as office doors).
  • vacuum, width brush attachment, or dry wipe all window blinds, shutters and treatments.
  • hand wash all reachable light fixtures, lamps and shades, and switch plates.
  • spot wash walls and or wallpaper.
  • wipe light switch plates, doors and doorframes for finger smudges.
  • hand wash all woodwork including baseboards, doors, banisters & cabinets.
  • hand wipe all nick-knacks
  • pictures and frames and hand wiped
  • stair railing will be hand wiped
  • edge all carpets and rugs with the crevice tool of the vacuum.
bathrooms
  • lighting will be disted and clear bulbs wiped clean
  • cabinet doors washed down completely.
  • baseboards washed.
kitchen
  • wash cabinet fronts.
  • clean inside of oven.
  • wash baseboards.
  • empty toaster crumbs and coffee grounds from machines.
  • clean and disinfect counter and wipe dry. scrub sink and wipe dry, scrubbing, clean stove area.
  • top of refrigerator hand washed.
  • above-cabinet decor hand wiped.
  • pot rack and accessories hand wiped.
optional add-ons include:
  • laundry
  • cleaning inside windows (as high up as we can reach on a two-step ladder).
